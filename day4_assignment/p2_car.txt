`2. Car Rental System`  
`Scenario:`  
A `car rental company` maintains records of available cars with `Car ID, Model, Rent Per Day`. The system should:  
- Use `unions` to store `either daily rent or total rent`  
- Save data in a `file`  
- Allow `updating rental information`  

`Input Example:`  
```
Enter number of cars: 3
Car 1: 401, Honda City, ₹1500/day
Car 2: 402, Ford EcoSport, ₹1800/day
Car 3: 403, Maruti Swift, ₹1200/day
```

`Output Example:`  
```
Cars Available:
1. Honda City - ₹1500/day
2. Ford EcoSport - ₹1800/day
3. Maruti Swift - ₹1200/day

Data saved in "car_rentals.txt"
```
