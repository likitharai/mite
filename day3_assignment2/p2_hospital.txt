`2. Hospital Emergency Room System`  
`Scenario:`  
A `hospital ER` maintains `N patient names 
with emergency levels` 
(1 to 5, where 5 is the highest). The system should:  
- Sort patients based on `emergency level (descending order)`  
- Display the `top 3 critical patients`  

Use `arrays and sorting`.

`Input Example:`  
```
Enter number of patients: 5
Patient Data:
1. Alice - Level 3
2. Bob - Level 5
3. Charlie - Level 2
4. David - Level 4
5. Eve - Level 5

Sorted by Emergency Level:
1. Bob - Level 5
2. Eve - Level 5
3. David - Level 4
4. Alice - Level 3
5. Charlie - Level 2

Top 3 Critical Patients:
1. Bob
2. Eve
3. David
```
